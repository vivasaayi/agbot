## Product direction
The platform is not positioned as a generic GIS application. It is positioned as an advisor-grade field intelligence platform for agriculture.

Primary initial users:
- agronomists
- crop consultants
- drone service providers

Core outcome:
- reduce time from imagery capture to actionable recommendation and shareable report

## Commercial stance
Do not optimize the core platform around a `$3/month` price point.

Use a tiered model:
- free or trial tier for evaluation
- starter tier for solo users
- professional tier for consultants and drone operators
- business tier for larger organizations

A `$3/month` offer can exist later as a farmer-viewer tier, but not as the primary monetization layer for a production product.
